8-Stage Water Purification Process

1. Coagulation

Coagulants bind dirt & impurities into bigger particles.

2. Sedimentation

Heavy particles settle at the bottom for removal.

3. Sand Filtration

Sand removes suspended particles, dust & turbidity.

4. Activated Carbon

Removes odor, chlorine, chemicals & improves taste.

5. Microfiltration

Tiny membrane removes bacteria & micro-impurities.

6. Reverse Osmosis (RO)

Purifies dissolved salts, heavy metals & contaminants.

7. UV Sterilization

Kills viruses & harmful microorganisms.

8. Ozonation

Final purification step ensuring 100% safe drinking water.
