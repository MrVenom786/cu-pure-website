About CU Pure

Our Mission

At CU Pure, our mission is to provide clean, safe, and eco-friendly drinking water to the students and staff of Chandigarh University. We aim to promote a healthy lifestyle while supporting sustainability by reducing plastic waste through reusable bottles.

Our Story

CU Pure was founded with the vision of creating a premium water bottle brand that caters to university students. Starting locally, our goal is to expand nationwide, offering high-quality water solutions for individuals, companies, hotels, and restaurants.
